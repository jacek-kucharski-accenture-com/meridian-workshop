# Executive Summary

**Proposal for:** Meridian Components, Inc. — RFP MC-2026-0417

---

Meridian Components operates a business-critical inventory dashboard that, by its own assessment, is incomplete. The previous vendor exited in November 2024 leaving known defects unresolved, no automated tests, and a codebase the IT team has been unwilling to touch since. Meanwhile, the Tokyo warehouse team works in English-only views, and operations staff lack the restocking tools they've been requesting for over a year.

We understand the core problem: Meridian needs a vendor who will finish the job, not scope around it.

Our approach is straightforward — audit first, test early, then build:

1. **Audit and remediate** the Reports module, resolving all defects identified through our own review of the codebase and any notes Meridian can provide.
2. **Establish test coverage** early in the engagement, so every subsequent change is safe to ship and IT can approve without manual review cycles.
3. **Build the Restocking view** — the feature R. Tanaka's team has been waiting for — with purchase order recommendations driven by live stock levels, demand forecasts, and operator-supplied budget ceilings.
4. **Deliver architecture documentation** suitable for handoff to Meridian IT, so institutional knowledge is no longer vendor-dependent.

We propose a fixed-fee engagement structured in four phases over eight weeks, with desired items (UI modernization, extended i18n, dark mode) available as defined add-ons within the $100K not-to-exceed envelope. Deliverables are phased so Meridian sees working software at the end of every two-week cycle — not a single handoff at the end.

We have done this before: inheriting under-documented codebases, establishing test coverage as a precondition for safe delivery, and building operations tooling that warehouse teams actually use. Our references are available on request.

The goal is a system Meridian's IT team can maintain and Meridian's operations team can rely on. That is the engagement we are proposing.
